# Consumer Safety Case Studies

This section documents real-life consumer safety incidents to highlight risks, lessons learned, and practical actions consumers can take to protect themselves.

The goal is not to assign blame, but to promote awareness, prevention, and informed decision-making.

## Why Case Studies Matter
Real stories help consumers:
- Recognize safety risks early
- Understand consequences of unsafe products
- Learn practical prevention strategies
- Appreciate the role of quality systems and regulation in public health

## Scope of Case Studies
Case studies may involve:
- Food safety incidents
- Counterfeit or fake products
- Product recalls
- Labeling and allergen failures
- Storage and handling issues

All cases are presented for educational purposes.
