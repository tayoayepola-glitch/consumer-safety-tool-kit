# Consumer Safety Toolkit

The Consumer Safety Toolkit is a global collection of practical resources designed to help consumers make safe, informed decisions when purchasing food and everyday consumer products.

## Why This Toolkit Exists
Unsafe, poorly stored, or counterfeit products pose serious risks to consumer health and safety worldwide. This toolkit simplifies safety checks and quality awareness, making them accessible to everyone—regardless of location.

## What You’ll Find in This Repository
- Food safety checks before and after purchase
- Identifying fake, substandard, or compromised products
- Understanding labels, expiry dates, and storage instructions
- Consumer rights and complaint handling basics
- Real-life case studies and safety lessons

## Who This Toolkit Is For
- Consumers globally
- Public health and consumer protection advocates
- Quality and food safety professionals
- Educators and community trainers

## How to Use This Toolkit
Explore the folders, download the guides, and apply the recommended checks in daily shopping and food handling practices.

## Scope and Disclaimer
This toolkit provides general consumer safety guidance. Local regulations and standards may vary by country, and users are encouraged to follow applicable national laws and official guidance.
